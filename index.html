Plan A Visit

Visiting a new church can be a scary experience. We want to put you at ease by answering the most common questions people have….

What are services like?

We sing hymns praising God for all he does, preaching lasts any where from 20 minutes to 40 minutes. We love church family fellowship, and love to see new people come to know Christ as their personal Lord and Savior!

When do you meet?

We meet every Sunday morning:

  • Sunday school starts at 9:30 Am
  • Regular service is at 10:30 Am
  • Evening service is at 6:30 PM
  • Wednesday evening starts at 6:30 PM

Is there anything for my kids?

We have a kids Sunday school. And we also have a Junior church during the morning sermon for kids ages 3-11. We also have a nursery avaliable for kids 0-3.

Where do you meet?

Our church is 4 miles south of Robinson on the west side of the road.
